About the online financial assessment

Send an email to your client with a secure link to the online assessment.

Your client will be asked to sign in to online banking and give one-time access to their bank accounts for 1 hour.

Your client will need to share all bank accounts, including any in joint names or with no money.

! How we use your client's details

  • We download 3 months of bank transactions
  • We only use information for the legal aid application
  • We automatically end the bank connection after 1 hour
  • We do not save online banking details
